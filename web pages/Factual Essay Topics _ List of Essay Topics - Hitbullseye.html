Top 100 Factual Essay Topics for 2019

Detailed list of important Essay Topics for 2019
Rate Us
Views:156391
Related: GDPIPrep Portal
Top 100 Factual Essay Topics for 2019
  • Describe the relevance of  BRICS and its future
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Cyber Security
  • How digitization is strengthening Indian Democracy?
  • Make in India- vs Incredible India
  • Advertisement is all glitter but no truth.
  • Cultural diversity hinders democratic governance.
  • Indian companies vs MNCs
  • Aadhaar Project and the Right to Privacy
  • Net Neutrality 
  • Internet censorship
  • Is India a really secular country?

  • Nuclear Energy- boon or bane

  • ISRO’s space missions - funds of country misused
  • Should India destroy the terrorist camps in Pakistan?
  • Should reservation in higher education be allowed?
  • Should Juvenile offenders be given a death penalty?
  • Facebook - A curse to personal space?
  • What is the impact of reduction in oil prices in the global economy?
  • Right to education: issues & implications
  • How to boost manufacturing sector in India?
  • Moral policing is an obstruction to country's art and culture
  • How should India be protected from global economic crisis?
  • How would you control farmer suicides?
  • What managers should learn from MS Dhoni?
  • Do celebrities have a responsibility towards consuming and believing in the products and causes they endorse?
  • Globalization is helping a lot of third world countries. Does it really help the underprivileged people who actually need it?
  • Is education being diluted by increasing number of seats and decreasing the course load?
  • Suicides in colleges are on an increase. Analyze and give comments regarding this trend. How would you act to counteract this?
  • Our PM has taken the initiative for skill building and vocational training. What steps will you follow to achieve this idea?
  • Make for India vs Make in India. Give your views.
  • Is the concept of paid news a threat to the integrity and honesty of print media?
  • Reality shows are escapism or entertainment?
  • What are the after effects of making euthanasia legal?
  • Should India have a uniform civil code?
  • What should be the priority of India in the 21st century - employment or entrepreneurship?
  • Is privatization the only way to bring down the real prices in the power & electricity industry?
  • Which is more important: experience or talent of the youth?
  • National heroes are created and destroyed by government. Should we revisit our history?
  • Regarding criminalization of Politics with so many criminals being selected and how can we stop it
  • Bullet Trains in India - pros and cons
  • People in college are eligible for voting, but they don’t register, what should be done?
  • Sexual violence against women has increased. News agencies report high profile cases. Are these genuine or hyped?
  • Demand for new states, is it justifiable?
  • Technology is a double edge sword. It should be managed carefully
  • Knowledge based economy is important to achieve the economic boom of India
  • Dowry system in India is curtailed only in law and not practically
  • Should government give more subsidies to farmers or provide better mechanism for loan?
  • The influence of western companies on Indian culture
  • India lives in villages but the villages don’t seem to live in India
  • Parliamentary democracy is obstructing the growth of India
  • Environmental concerns should take the highest priority in people's minds
  • Lower voter turnout: A blot on democracy?
  • Swachh Bharat Campaign. Success or failure?
  • #MeToo
  • Harms of increasing trend of social media. Suggest solutions. 
  • People do not verify the validity of information before posting opinions, facts, etc on social media. What problems can this lead to? Suggest steps to improve this situation. Give examples.
  • Mob censorship challenges artistic freedom and rights of creative people. Should a writer be allowed to express his / her views, even if they oppose views of some individuals or groups? Give your views with examples.
  • Why ‘Make in India’ when we can buy cheap material from China?
  • Primary Education v/s Secondary Education- Should the government of India builds more IIMs or should it focus on building primary schools?
  • Political parties should be funded by state and not by industrialists or public donations.
  • Contribution of agriculture in Indian economy
  • Start-up culture: a fad or a game changer?
  • Strategies to improve sports in India.
  • India should move to a Presidential form of Government. 
  • National security is a priority over right to privacy.
  • Ways to control pollution in Delhi.
  • Solar power is the energy source of the future.
  • Is population of India an asset or liability?
  • Impact of social media on Indian society
  • Domestic violence is an outcome of lack of education
  • Outsourcing in India is shifting from services to manufacturing
  • E-Commerce is the face of the new economy
  • Women make better managers than men: comment
  • India and China- Enemies or Friends?
  • How businesses can survive and thrive in the current times
  • Low Agricultural Productivity. Suggest ways to increase productivity?
  • Can India become an economic super power till 2020?
  • Gender sensitivity needs special attention from childhood
  • Online learning is more efficient than traditional learning. Your views?
  • Cricket, Cinema, Culture & Politics: turning points of India
  • According to UN studies, Indian masses have more access to cell phones rather than basic sanitation. Point out reasons and give solutions
  • IPL - has cricket lost its essence
  • Are we heading towards a new era of Indian Politics?
  • Pvt. Health insurance in India “Advantage and Disadvantage”
  • Global Warming: reality or myth
  • Indian Judicial System: from here to where
  • Can online shopping eradicate shopping malls?
  • Challenges faced in implementing e-learning in India
  • Should highly paid employees enjoy protection under labor laws?
  • Is India taking adequate advantage of decreasing oil prices to reduce fiscal deficit?
  • Literary Freedom: What is the limit?
  • Environment - What man is doing to Nature
  • Merits and Demerits of cashless economy.
  • After demonetization what next steps should be taken to clean up the system?
  • With the advent of photography techniques do you think natural zoo's can be replace by virtual zoo's? What are the impacts on zoo's if such thing happens?
  • “Some people consider nuclear weapons as a deterrent to war. While others claim that they’re dangerous and hence of no use”. What is your opinion?
  • Building monuments on the names of great leader is a waste of public money.
  • Corruption and Poverty go hand in hand.
  •  Private sector promotes employability whereas public sector promotes employment.
