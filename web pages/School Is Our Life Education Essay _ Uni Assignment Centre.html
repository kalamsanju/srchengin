School Is Our Life Education Essay

2. First school

3. First teachers

4. Today’s school

5. My school

School

Every day every student go to school and learn, but not every student thinks what it means and how did the first school form. Nowadays many people think that school is just facility, where children learn something. But it is not true, because school is our life. Every day people learn something new. In the school where we are taught by teachers, in life we are taught to different people, for example, our parents, our friends or someone else. So, what school means? School - is the place where you make your first steps, where you first get acquainted with such feelings as confidence, responsibility, learn mobility and resourcefulness, take initiative, make friends and enemies, and most importantly - look for yourself, your way .... so, my question is how school did come?

Many centuries ago, people were not trained group, sometimes to the teacher came only one student, because there was no classes or groups. Also, in those days people did not have diaries. Teachers did not put any marks on the lesson. They rated students to themself, to know their levels. The very first school appeared in Greece and Rome. In those days, people were taught more spirituality and astronomy, they been investigating natural phenomena. Every night scientists looked to the telescope to see night sky, which consists of bright moon, enigmatic stars, other planets, that stays near our planet Earth. While some people believed that the earth was round the other believed that it is flat. So, those times was very interesting to discover something new about people, Earth, philosophy, astronomy and so one.

Once upon a time, when there was no division of labor, teachers could be the oldest people, more experienced representatives of the tribe / community. In the transition to the division of labor, the teachers were people who practiced a particular craft and passed on a particular skill / knowledge. It was only in the 18th - 19th centuries, the teaching profession was recognized as officially profession in Europe and other peace of the world ...Teaching is the most ancient and essential. The teacher's work is not easy, but it is honorable. First teacher was Socrate. I think, he was philosophy teacher, because he always thought about life, how to live, how to know what is bad and what is good. The main idea of ​​the philosophy of Socrates - self-knowledge as the path to wisdom. Once he said: "I do not regret the time, because I do not take the money, I teach for free". So, in my opinion, teachers should teach from the heart with pleasure. Bad person is person who does not like his profession.

Nowadays there are about 100 schools in Astana and 7755 [1] schools in Kazakhstan. It is very different number of schools between the ancient world and nowadays. Today's generation of children is very happy, because today children in school can use computers, books in big and rich libraries, get marks, take more knowledge. There is set of conditions in order to learn well. Today children study 6 times a week. At home they do their homework.

So, what about my school? I am a student of Nazarbaev Intellectual School. My School - it is very spacious, bright and large building. Each room is equipped with various appliances and furniture. But the school is not just a building where you study. School is life, because there are most of my friends here. This is where people can help you and explain. School also can be compared to a bridge that connects my childhood with adulthood. When I go across the bridge with every step I know something new. In my school every child wears school uniform. I think it is comfortable, beautiful and stylish clothing. At school we eat three times a day. Our chefs prepare tasty food. Also, students can develop their talents here. There many classes in our school. For example, dance club, art classes, music classes, debaters club, volleyball and others. I go to dance studio. It help me to save my form and be energy and plastic. I like it. In my opinion, if I am a student at the school named after the president, I must comply with all the indicators and implement plans of Nursultan Nazarbaev.

Teachers are people that can help make the right choices, they teach, conduct. All this, in order that in the future we became real people and were able to select suitable jobs. A challenge student is to listen carefully and to keep all the knowledge in his head. In my school there are many experienced, intelligent and good teachers. They always help me and other students to understand new topic. Skill teachers in the classroom is primarily a skillful possession of a training and education, the creative application of new achievement of good pedagogy and teaching experience, management of cognitive and practical activities of students, their intellectual development. I think that the work of a teacher is very difficult. They should prepare materials on a particular topic lesson. Check our work. But we should to look at the teacher as a person first and then by profession. So we can say that the teacher is a great profession.

Education is one of the main points of a person's life. In ancient times, people have come to the school to rest, as in other days they worked. It does not seem to present days. Nowadays student should study with excellent marks and then in the future to get a high-paying job. I know it because of history. Nowadays no more think about today’s history, notices how the time and our achievements remain in school history. So we need to think about the present and think about the prosperity and future. School helps to understand the value of life. Also it promotes the development of students' knowledge and abilities, increase enthusiasm for learning. Teachers guide students in the right direction, the students discover the world on the other hand, the stock of knowledge helps in the future to be good people
