Persuasive Essay Topics: 10 Ideas for a Winning Essay on Education

Date: December 29, 2016
  • Home
  • / Blog
  • / Persuasive Essay Topics: 10 Ideas for a Winning Essay on Education

A persuasive essay is an essay that uses reason along with logic so the person reading it can see that one idea is more justifiable than another idea. The purpose of a persuasive essay is to influence the reader to take on a particular viewpoint or to adopt a certain action. It is imperative that the persuasive essay has solid evidence within it and sound reasoning.

HOW TO WRITE A PERSUASIVE ESSAY

To write a persuasive essay is not a big deal since a persuasive essay outline is simple to follow: introduction, body paragraphs, and conclusion.

10 Persuasive Essay Topics on Education

1. Grades don’t indicate either intelligence or effort. 

2. The lack of early childhood education is a big gap in the skill levels of children. 

3. Secondary education should not increase unemployment. 

4. College classes should start later. 

5. Education should inspire. 

6. Should employees or be able to claim a deduction for self-education expenses? 

7. Online learning can’t replace face-to-face education. 

8. Teaching handwriting isn’t important anymore.  

9. Why is post-secondary education is important?  

10. Students should be able to choose teachers.
